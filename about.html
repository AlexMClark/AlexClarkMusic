About

Hello! I'm Alex Clark, a commercial composer and songwriter.

I've been composing for Radio/Television/Web and more for 8+ years and have worked with some of the largest advertising agencies in the world. I was born and raised in Louisville, KY, though I lived in New York City for several years after attending Indiana University for college. I returned to Louisville in 2010, and recently released an album under the name Toy Tiger.

If you would like to follow me on facebook, my page is Alex Clark Music and you would like to follow me on twitter, my username is @alexclarkmusic.
